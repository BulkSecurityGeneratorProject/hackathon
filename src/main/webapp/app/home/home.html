MrEd (Multi-route Equine De-confliction)

Military aircraft will avoid over-flying horse riders if they can be identified in sufficient time, and if it is safe to do so. In particular, aircrew are trained to identify and avoid potential conflicts. However, conducting violent avoiding manoeuvres when close to riders significantly increases the potential for disturbance. So, if riders are seen late, military aircraft will usually over-fly without manoeuvring as this is the safest option

MrEd automates this process leading to a safer experience all round.
